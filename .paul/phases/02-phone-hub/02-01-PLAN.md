<objective>
## Goal
Consolidate Calls, Campaigns, and Assistants into a single `/phone` page with three tabs.
Remove the three separate sidebar nav entries and replace with one "Phone" item.

## Purpose
Currently the sidebar has three separate entries (Calls, Campaigns, Assistants) that all relate
to telephony. Grouping them under one "Phone" hub reduces cognitive load and makes the nav cleaner.
Each tab preserves 100% of its existing functionality — this is a routing/layout change, not a redesign.

## Output
- `/phone` page with tabs: Calls | Campaigns | Assistants
- Active tab driven by `?tab=calls|campaigns|assistants` (default: calls)
- `/calls`, `/outbound`, `/assistants` routes redirect to the appropriate `/phone?tab=` URL
- Sidebar: Calls + Campaigns + Assistants removed, "Phone" added (single entry, href="/phone")
- Deep links `/calls/[callId]` and `/outbound/[id]` remain functional (not moved)
</objective>
